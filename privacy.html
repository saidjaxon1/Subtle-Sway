Privacy Policy

Subtle Sway is a small static website, and we have tried to keep it as simple and privacy-respecting as possible. This page explains what happens to your information when you visit.

What we collect

We do not run accounts, forms, or logins on this site, and we do not directly collect personal information such as your name or email address. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may keep standard server logs — for example IP addresses — for security and reliability. We do not have access to these logs in a way that identifies you.

Cookies and affiliate links

This site itself does not set advertising or tracking cookies. However, our affiliate links lead to third-party retailers such as Amazon. When you follow one of these links, that retailer may set cookies to record the referral and any purchase you make — this is how affiliate commissions work. Please refer to the privacy policy of the retailer for details on how they handle your data.

Third-party content

To display the site, we load fonts from Google Fonts and images from Unsplash. These providers may receive your IP address as part of serving those files. We do not control how they process that information; their own privacy policies apply.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new date above.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out through our contact page.
